Qencrypt Mobile — Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how FiveRivers Technologies Pvt Ltd collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the Qencrypt mobile application for iOS.

1. Who we are

Controller: FiveRivers Technologies Pvt Ltd
Product: Qencrypt — encryption and vault software for your personal files on your device.
Contact: support@qencrypt.app

2. Scope

This policy applies to the Qencrypt iOS App. It does not govern third-party websites or services you open from the App.

3. Information the App processes

3.1 Information you provide or that is created on your device

  • Account and authentication: If you create or use an account, we may process identifiers you provide and authentication-related data.
  • Vault and encryption: Your encrypted files, vault contents, master password, and cryptographic keys are stored and processed primarily on your device.
  • Recovery and keys: Recovery codes, key export/import, or backup features are handled according to the feature.
  • Settings and preferences: The App stores preferences on the device.

3.2 Information collected automatically

We use analytics and diagnostic logging to understand stability, improve the product, and measure feature usage. This data may be sent to PostHog when the device is online.

  • App version/build, device model, and operational event data.
  • Authentication-related analytics where account identifiers may be included.
  • Operational messages such as vault actions, counts, timing, and non-content error reasons.

3.3 Apple In-App Purchases and subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple. FiveRivers does not receive your full payment card number.

3.4 Permissions on your device

The App may request access to Photo Library, Face ID / Touch ID, and Notifications where required for features.

4. How we use information

  • To provide and maintain the App.
  • To authenticate you and protect accounts.
  • To improve the App and fix issues.
  • To comply with law and respond to lawful requests.
  • To enforce our terms and protect users and the service.

5. Legal bases

Where applicable, we rely on performance of a contract, legitimate interests, consent, and legal obligation.

6. Sharing and subprocessors

We may share information with PostHog, infrastructure and IT vendors, Apple, and authorities where required by law.

7. International transfers

Your information may be transferred internationally, including to the United States, with appropriate safeguards where required.

8. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described and as required by law.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object, port data, or withdraw consent. Email support@qencrypt.app to exercise rights.

11. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 or the age required in your jurisdiction.

12. Third-party links

The App may contain links to third-party sites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

13. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy and indicate the new version by the “Last updated” date.
